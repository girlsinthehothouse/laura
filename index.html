Electro-Pop / hyperpop

FIAMMA slays Turbo Haus. YASSSSS

by John E. Mitchell

Henny, let me tell you about a night so electrifying, so gag-worthy, so dripping in feminine excellence that even Mother Nature had to clutch her pearls and say, “Shantay, you stay!” 🌈✨

Montreal spring is serving full-on Gemini energy, flipping the script faster than a lip-sync smackdown. One minute, the sun is strutting the runway, melting winter’s tired old wig into a tragic lace-front reveal, and the next—BAM!—a shady little squall sashays in, throwing snow, dampness, and just a touch of disrespect, like a trade ghosting you after a “wyd?” text. But guess what, darling? None of that mattered. Turbo Haus on Saturday night was hotter than a wig under stage lights, thanks to a lineup of electro-pop divas snatching wigs, edges, and souls—Storylike, Franki, Hot House, and most importantly… FIAMMA. Baby, these queens didn’t just come to play; they came to slay, and FIAMMA? Oh, she devoured the competition, leaving nothing but crumbs and a perfectly arched eyebrow in her wake. Crown her Canada’s Next Electro-Pop Superstar and back up, because this queen is coming for global domination. 👑💅🔥

FIAMMA as fuckkkkk

Kicking off the evening was FIAMMA, a celestial goddess teleported straight from the electro-pop cosmos, shimmering with enough star power to make even the Milky Way feel underdressed. Hailing from Ottawa and Montreal, this diva came dripping in dreamy leftfield synth-pop fantasy, eating those bedroom pop bitches up, and is ready to snatch the scene bald and leave it begging for more. And let’s make one thing crystal-clear: is she still finding her groove? Oh, helllllll to the no, honey! This queen has more groove than a Studio 54 dance floor, and she’s serving it with a side of slay.

Let’s talk pipes—because FIAMMA’s vocals? Pure honey-dipped, angel-chorus, hair-raising bliss. She doesn’t sing; she enchants. She hypnotizes. She summons spirits from the depths of the disco inferno and commands them to dance. Tracks like “Wish U Were Here” showcase her melodic sorcery, adorned with ethereal backing vocals, a sprinkle of drama, and a full-bodied, cinematic moment of pop euphoria—like the final key change in a power ballad, but make it electro-glam realness.

And let’s get one thing straight: this unstoppable queen isn’t just riding the pop train—she’s driving the damn thing. She doesn’t need another ounce of charisma, uniqueness, nerve, or talent—because baby, she’s already overflowing. Okuuuurrrt?! 💅🔥

Mark my words—this icon-in-the-making is about to blow up harder than a Drag Race makeover episode on steroids. So get your life, set your wig glue on extra strength, and stream her 2024 ‘Hurry Up and Wait’ EP—because if you miss this moment, you might as well pack your bags and go home, because FIAMMA is IT, baby. 💥🎶👑
